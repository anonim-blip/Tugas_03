Briards

A HEART WRAPPED IN FUR

The briard, or berger de bride, is a large breed of dog traditionally used as a herder and guardian of sheep.

Breed History

The biard, which is believed to have originated in France, has been bred for centuries to herd and to protect sheep. The breed was used b the French Army as sentries, messengers and to search for wounded soldiers because of its fine sense of hearing
